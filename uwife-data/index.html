UKIRT Widefield Infrared Survey for [Fe II]

We have imaged about 180 square degrees along the Galactic Plane (6$^{\circ}$ < l < 65$^{\circ}$; -1.5$^{\circ}$ < b < +1.5$^{\circ}$) with WFCAM at UKIRT. The observations are taken with the narrow band filter centred on the atomic Iron emission line ([Fe II] 1.64$\mu$m) with an integration time of 720 sec per pixel.
